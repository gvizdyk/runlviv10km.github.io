Lvivska desiatka

"Lvivska desiatka" - is a traditional long-distance road running competition over a distance of 10 kilometres (6.2 miles). Ukrainian Athletic Federation granted this competition the status of Championship of Ukraine. All running enthusiasts can compete along with the professionals!

The competition starts at 9 am, the award ceremony is at 11.30 am.

To participate in the competition you need:

Statement of good health

Statement of good health (medical certificate), that confirms satisfactory state of your health, is obligatory. Also we require a signed statement, which indicates that each participant takes part in competition at their own risk and responsibility.

Entry fee

You can pay the entry fee: using bank card via LIQPAY system before September 6, 2015, or in the Secretariat of the competition on September 19-20, 2015. Entry fee is 130 UAH before 20 July 2015.

Age

Any person who is 18 years or older on the day of the event (20 September 2015) can compete.

Provision

On the conduction of the running competition over a distance of 10 kilometres down the highway "Lvivska desiatka".

Regulations

Regulated time to overcome distance - 1 hour 20 min. Participants who will be unable to cover the distance in regulated time must take off their bib number and leave the competition route.

Health care

The competition organizers will provide medical services to the participants during the competition (doctor will be at the start and the finish). Route will be accompanied by an ambulance.

The route and running distance

Route on stage approval

The time will be measured using electronic system.
Distance - 10 kilometres (6.2 miles).

The route will be marked, as well as every kilometer of the distance. Volunteers and judges will help to organize movement on the road. The competition program also contains additional races for children and charity events.

Registration

Each competitor must register for the competition by passing preliminary e-registration, or directly in the Secretariat of the competition that will work on September 19-20, 2015.

Admission of additional applications, registration of participants, and issuance of bib numbers will be held on September 19, Saturday at 16 pm during the Pasta Party and on the day of the competition, September 20, 2015, before the competition start from 9 am to 11 am.

The final application for participation in the competition is: pre-registration, medical certificate, the signed statement, the entry fee payment.

Entry fee

You can pay the entry fee: using bank card via LIQPAY system before September 6, 2015, or in the Secretariat of the competition on September 19-20, 2015.
